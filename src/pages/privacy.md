JackieSung is designed to be simple and static.

## What is collected

By default, this skeleton does not include comments, accounts, newsletters, or analytics. Your hosting provider may still
collect basic server logs such as IP address, browser type, requested URL, and timestamp.

## Email

If you email [hello@example.com](mailto:hello@example.com), your message and email address will be used only to reply.

## Changes

Update this page before adding analytics, forms, newsletters, comments, affiliate tracking scripts, or any other
third-party service.
