JackieSung is a static website and does not intentionally set first-party cookies for accounts, comments, newsletters,
or advertising.

## Essential cookies

No first-party essential cookies are required for the site to load.

## Third-party services

This site may link to third-party websites. If you follow those links, the destination site may use its own cookies,
tracking pixels, or similar technologies under its own policy.

## Analytics

No analytics cookies are configured by default. This page should be updated before adding analytics, embedded widgets,
comments, newsletters, or other third-party services that set cookies.

## Managing cookies

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings. Blocking cookies should not prevent this static site from
loading.
